Third Pole E Base

Built in the Mahabodhi School campus in Leh, the E-Base is a fully equipped digital classroom, connected to the outside world with Wimax. It's an initiative focused on helping the children of Ladakh take on the world.

Village Microgrid Project

An ongoing project, to design and erect a microgrid , first in one pilot village, and later in more remote villages of Himalayas. The idea is to make the region self-sufficient in energy, one village at a time.
