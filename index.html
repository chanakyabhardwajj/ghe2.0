Global Himalayan Expedition

9th - 21st August 2015

Third Pole E-Base

Built in the Mahabodhi School campus in Leh, the E-Base is a fully equipped digital classroom, connected to the outside world with Wimax. It's an initiative focused on helping the children of Ladakh take on the world.
read more...

Himalayan Village Electrification

This August, Global Himalayan Expedition would be electrifying another village in the Himalayan valley. The expedition participants would be involved in setting up the Solar Microgrid and providing Clean light to the entire village.
